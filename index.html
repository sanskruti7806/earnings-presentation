Financial Math

Net Present Value (NPV):

$$\mathrm{NPV}=\sum_{t=0}^{n}\frac{C_t}{(1+r)^t}$$

Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR):

$$\mathrm{CAGR}=\left(\frac{\text{Ending}}{\text{Beginning}}\right)^{1/n}-1$$

Briefly define each term: \(C_t\) = cash flow at time \(t\), \(r\) = discount rate.
